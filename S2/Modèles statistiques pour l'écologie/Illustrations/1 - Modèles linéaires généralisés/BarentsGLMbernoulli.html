Logistic regression model for the abundance

Setting \(x_{i0} \equiv 1\) and \(\beta = [\beta_0 \dots \beta_d]^\intercal\), \[ Y_i \sim \mathcal{B}(\pi_i), \qquad \log\frac{\pi_i}{1-\pi_i} = x_i^\intercal \beta. \]
